Visual identity is the first handshake between a brand and its customers. This logo was developed for **Arı Kırtasiye** during my high school internship. The objective was to create a memorable, approachable, and industry-relevant brand mark that resonates with students and office professionals alike.

---

## 🚀 Design Philosophy

The project focuses on **Mascot-Centric Branding**:

*   **Mascot Illustration:** Developing a friendly, industrious bee character to symbolize the hard-working nature of the company and to appeal to a younger demographic.
*   **Geometric Framing:** Utilizing concentric circular paths to encase the typography, ensuring the logo remains balanced and legible across various media (business cards, signage, digital headers).
*   **Color Theory:** A high-contrast palette of **Orange and Black** was chosen to evoke energy, productivity (orange), and professional stability (black).
*   **Circular Typography:** Implementing an arc-text path for "KIRTASİYE" to maintain visual harmony with the mascot's dynamic posture.

## 🌟 Key Features

*   **Custom Mascot Design:** A unique character that acts as the brand ambassador.
*   **Visual Hierarchy:** Strategic placement of the "ARI" text to ensure immediate brand recognition.
*   **Scalable Architecture:** Designed with clear boundaries for high-fidelity reproduction on physical stationery and large-scale banners.
